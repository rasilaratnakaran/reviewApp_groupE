Camera

The iPhone 13 and 13 mini have a dual-camera system with a 12MP ultra-wide and wide lens. The iPhone 13 Pro and Pro Max have a triple-camera system with a 12MP ultra-wide, wide, and telephoto lens, as well as a LiDAR scanner for improved autofocus and AR experiences. All models have Night mode, Deep Fusion, and Smart HDR 4 for better low-light photography.

Performance

The iPhone 13 is powered by Apple's A15 Bionic chip, which is said to be the fastest chip ever in a smartphone. It has 5G connectivity for faster internet speeds and improved performance. It also has improved battery life, with the iPhone 13 mini lasting up to 1.5 hours longer than the iPhone 12 mini, and the iPhone 13 Pro Max lasting up to 2.5 hours longer than the iPhone 12 Pro Max.

Other features

The iPhone 13 runs on iOS 15, which comes with new features such as Focus modes, Live Text, and FaceTime improvements. It also has MagSafe technology for improved wireless charging and accessories. It comes in new colors such as Pink and Sierra Blue, as well as traditional colors like Gold and Silver.

Price and availability

The iPhone 13 starts at £799 for the iPhone 13 mini, £999 for the iPhone 13, £1,099 for the iPhone 13 Pro, and £1,199 for the iPhone 13 Pro Max. It is now available for pre-order and will be released on September 24, 2021.
